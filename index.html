"Lose 30 pounds in 30 days"

"Earn $10,000 for filling out this survey"


Those claims are always so enticing, aren't they! It would be nice if it were that easy.

This site won't help you if you don't put the time in to practice. With diet, and exercise it's possible that you could lose 30 lbs in 30 days!

Just like ONLY reading diet tips, NO action will result in NO changes

Read a chapter in a java book.

Do a problem.

Re-read the chapter.

Do additional exercises.

Seems like a lot of work doesn't it. But to truly understand to the core of what's happening, you need to take the time to ask questions of how and why things work in java. Otherwise, you will merely know the very surface of things, and who wants that!

I suggest spending an hour a day doing exercises/reading to reinforce those exercises. Forego the hour of reailty tv, it's worth it!
